# Product and AI Plan

## Product Positioning

Family Tripwise should be a planning assistant for family trips, not a generic AI travel blog.

The site should help parents make decisions that static content handles poorly:

- Which neighborhood is best for our family?
- Which hotel problems matter for kids?
- How much walking is too much?
- What can we do between naps?
- What if it rains?
- Which activities fit a toddler and a teen?

## Core Tools

### 1. Where-to-Stay Recommender

Inputs:

- Destination
- Kid ages
- Budget
- Trip length
- Hotel vs apartment preference
- Car vs transit
- Stroller needs
- Pool/breakfast/kitchen needs

Outputs:

- Recommended areas
- Avoid-if notes
- Hotel/apartment fit
- Transit difficulty
- Nearby activities

### 2. Family Hotel Review Summarizer

Inputs:

- Hotel name or URL
- Family priorities

Outputs:

- Room-size summary
- Noise risk
- Pool notes
- Breakfast/kitchen notes
- Elevator/stroller notes
- Recent complaint patterns
- Family-fit score

### 3. Nap-Friendly Itinerary Builder

Inputs:

- Destination
- Trip length
- Kid ages
- Wake/nap/bedtime windows
- Transit preference
- Must-do attractions

Outputs:

- Day-by-day itinerary
- Rest windows
- Backup options
- Map grouping
- Walking/transit load

### 4. Rainy-Day Backup Planner

Inputs:

- Destination
- Date/season
- Kid ages
- Indoor/outdoor preferences

Outputs:

- Indoor activities
- Booking-needed warnings
- Nearby food/rest stops

### 5. Packing List Generator

Inputs:

- Destination
- Season
- Kid ages
- Airline/travel mode
- Lodging type

Outputs:

- Family packing checklist
- Stroller/car seat notes
- Weather-specific items

## AI Safety and Trust Rules

- Do not invent personal experience.
- Show confidence levels for generated recommendations.
- Require human review before publishing high-impact lodging, safety, or transit guidance.
- Keep source timestamps where possible.
- Separate verified data from inferred recommendations.

## Differentiation Against Blogs

Static blogs answer: "Here are things we did."

Family Tripwise should answer: "Given your children, budget, trip length, mobility needs, and lodging style, here is the plan that will actually work."
