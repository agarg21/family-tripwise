# Domain Name Research

Date checked: July 2, 2026

Method:

- `.com` registry RDAP checks against Verisign RDAP.
- DNS A-record checks with `dig`.
- A `404` RDAP response is treated as `appears available`, not as a purchase guarantee.

Important caveat: domain availability can change at any moment, and final registration should be verified at a registrar before making branding decisions.

## Naming Criteria

Good names for this project should be:

- Parent-friendly and practical.
- Broad enough for destinations, hotels, itineraries, and tools.
- Not overly tied to AI.
- Easy to say and spell.
- Suitable for a trusted travel-planning product.

## Recommendation

The strongest initial brand/domain choice is `familytripwise.com`.

Reasons:

- It matches the product promise: smarter family trip decisions.
- It can cover destinations, hotels, itineraries, maps, and AI tools.
- It does not lock the brand into toddlers only.
- It avoids sounding like a thin AI content site.

Backup: `littletripguide.com` if the brand should feel warmer and more editorial.
