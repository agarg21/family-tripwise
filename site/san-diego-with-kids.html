3-day starter plan

Nap-friendly San Diego itinerary

  1. Day 1: Mission Bay and easy arrival Short beach time, groceries, pool, early dinner.
  2. Day 2: Zoo plus Balboa Park Zoo early, rest after lunch, one low-pressure museum or garden walk.
  3. Day 3: La Jolla or Coronado Choose La Jolla for scenery or Coronado for a calmer beach day.
