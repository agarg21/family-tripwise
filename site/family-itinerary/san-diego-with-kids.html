San Diego, California

San Diego itinerary with kids

A starter family itinerary for San Diego with morning, afternoon, and evening pacing, rainy-day swaps, and age adjustments.

3-day starter plan

Nap-friendly structure

  1. Day 1Mission Bay or hotel pool, groceries, early dinner, and a low-pressure arrival.
  2. Day 2Zoo early, rest after lunch, then one Balboa Park garden or museum.
  3. Day 3Choose La Jolla for scenery or Coronado for a calmer beach-and-ferry day.

Adjust by age

For toddlers, shorten the morning anchor and protect the post-lunch reset. For teens, swap one playground or short museum stop for a more independent neighborhood, food, tour, or outdoor activity.

Starter plan only. Adjust by season, weather, child needs, opening hours, and human-reviewed safety/transit notes.

2-day option

Condensed weekend plan

  1. Day 1Mission Bay or hotel pool, groceries, early dinner, and a low-pressure arrival.
  2. Day 2Zoo early, rest after lunch, then one Balboa Park garden or museum.

Weekend rule

Skip one ambitious add-on and protect either the hotel reset or the easiest dinner. A two-day family trip works better when one activity is allowed to be optional.
