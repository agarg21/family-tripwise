Las Vegas itinerary with kids

A starter family itinerary for Las Vegas with morning, afternoon, and evening pacing, rainy-day swaps, and age adjustments.

Primary keyword signal: 10.

3-day starter plan

Nap-friendly structure

  1. Day 1Hotel pool, short Conservatory/fountain loop, early dinner.
  2. Day 2Choose a kid-friendly show or museum, rest, then High Roller or a short evening spectacle.
  3. Day 3Red Rock Canyon for older kids or a second pool-and-museum day for younger kids.

Adjust by age

For toddlers, shorten the morning anchor and protect the post-lunch reset. For teens, swap one playground or short museum stop for a more independent neighborhood, food, tour, or outdoor activity.

Starter plan only. Adjust by season, weather, child needs, opening hours, and human-reviewed safety/transit notes.
